About KidsInspiring Nation

We are a faith-based ministry dedicated to raising children who will build nations through spiritual depth, skilled excellence, and servant leadership.

Our Vision

That KIND (The KidsInspiring Nation Devotional) is sounded in every family with a child across the Nations of the World.

Our Mission

To empower the younger generation through three transformative pillars: Develop Spirit, Deploy Skills, and Deliver Service.

Our Values

Faith in Jesus Christ, Excellence in Service, Love for Community, Integrity in Character, and Joy in the Lord.

Our Story

KidsInspiring Nation (KIN) was born out of a divine mandate to raise children as goDs - powerful, purposeful, and positioned to build nations. We believe that every child carries the potential to change the world through faith in Jesus Christ.

Our journey began with a simple question: "How can we consistently disciple children in a way that transforms not just their lives, but entire nations?" The answer came through the revelation of the 3 De's framework.

Through daily devotionals (KIND), weekly services (goDs Experience), values education (Psalm 119, PRISM Challenge), and comprehensive development (goDs University), we're creating a generation that knows God, walks in their gifts, and serves with excellence.

Today, we reach hundreds of families across multiple nations through Telegram, YouTube, and social media. Our community is growing, but our mission remains the same: Raising goDs, Building NATIONS.

The 3 De's Framework

1. Develop Your Spirit

We believe spiritual development is the foundation of everything. Through Bible study, prayer, worship, and devotionals, children learn to:

  • Read and understand the Bible
  • Pray effectively and hear God's voice
  • Fast and practice spiritual disciplines
  • Worship with authenticity and passion
  • Walk in relation with the Holy Spirit

2. Deploy Your Skills

God has given every child unique gifts and talents. We help them discover, develop, and deploy these skills through:

  • Music and worship leading
  • Public speaking and communication
  • Creative arts and expression
  • Leadership development
  • Talent showcases and platforms

3. Deliver Your Service

True greatness is found in service. We teach children to make a positive impact through:

  • Community service projects
  • Acts of kindness and compassion
  • National values education
  • Purpose discovery and calling
  • Nation-building initiatives
